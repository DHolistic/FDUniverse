## 📏 **UPDATED TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS**

### **PAGE DIMENSIONS:**
- **Trim size**: 8.5" × 11" (landscape orientation)
- **Bleed area**: 8.75" × 11.25" (0.125" bleed on all sides)
- **Safe area**: 8.0" × 10.5" (0.25" margin from trim)
- **Gutter margin**: 1.0" (binding side)
- **Outside margins**: 0.5" (top, bottom, outside)

### **IMAGE SPECIFICATIONS:**
- **Resolution**: 300 DPI minimum
- **Right page image size**: ~7.5" × 10.0" (full bleed potential)
- **Color profile**: CMYK for professional printing
- **File format**: High-resolution PNG or TIFF

### **TEXT SPECIFICATIONS:**
- **Left page text area**: ~6.5" × 9.5"
- **Font size**: 12-14pt for body text (larger format allows bigger fonts)
- **Line spacing**: 1.5× for comfortable reading
- **Paragraph spacing**: 18pt between paragraphs

## 🎨 **ENHANCED VISUAL OPPORTUNITIES**

### **LARGER FORMAT BENEFITS:**
- **Character detail**: Facial expressions, clay textures more visible
- **Environmental complexity**: More architectural detail, atmospheric effects
- **Consciousness energy**: Blue consciousness glyphs and energy more detailed
- **Dramatic lighting**: Subtle gradations, complex shadow work
- **Sacred text integration**: Larger, more readable glyph presentations

### **PREMIUM PRODUCTION VALUES:**
- **Paper quality**: High-quality paper stock (at least 120gsm)
- **Color reproduction**: Professional offset printing for best results
- **Binding**: Perfect bound or case bound for durability
- **Cover design**: Full-color wraparound cover with spot effects

## 💰 **COFFEE TABLE BOOK ECONOMICS**

### **PRICING ADVANTAGES:**
- **Higher cover price**: $25-45 vs. $15-25 for standard graphic novels
- **Gift market**: Coffee table books are popular gift items
- **Collector appeal**: Premium format attracts serious readers
- **Library sales**: Educational institutions prefer quality formats

### **PRODUCTION CONSIDERATIONS:**
- **Print costs**: ~$8-15 per book for professional printing
- **Minimum quantities**: Usually 100-250 books minimum
- **Shipping weight**: Heavier format increases shipping costs
- **Storage**: Larger format requires more storage space
